What is iSPACE?

iSPACE means Introduction to Systematic Programming, Animation, Computer Engineering, and Electronics.

iSPACE is designed to help young students develop innovative and creative abilities that will be useful in many areas of learning and problem solving in their society and carrier path from a young age.
